Hosting a Jam Site

We had over 30 locations worldwide last year, but we'd love to add more! It can take a lot of time and resources to host your own jam site, as you'll need to provide:

  • workspace
  • wireless internet access
  • livestream camera
  • projector or large televsion
  • parking or nearby public transit access
  • nearby access to restaurants

Although not a requirement, some locations secure sponsors that provide:

  • snacks and drinks
  • t-shirts
  • large meeting space

Popular locations to host a jam include:

  • co-working spaces
  • game studios
  • colleges and universities

Get in touch and let us know how many people you can accommodate at your site, the physical location, and any other information you think we should know about, and thanks for helping us save the games industry!
